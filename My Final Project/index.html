    About Me

    Hi! My name is Dana. I created this website using what I learned during my Front-End Develepment courses at General Assembly. They were super fun and now I feel like I'm smart or something! My website was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.

    A little about myself - I have a puppy named Beemo (pictured above) and she's really cute. When I'm not telling people how much I love my dog, I love to draw/illustrate things in a comical way. Comical to me, at least.

    Other than that, I'm a pretty simple lady. Just give me a good book, a hot cup o'joe, and strongly magnifying binoculars to peer into my neighbor's window from 1 to 4 am every night. What is he dreaming about? Must be something really lovely and relaxing to be looking so peaceful like that. Maybe he is dreaming of a past love. Maybe he's dreaming of a new love. Someone who would treat him right, not that like cold shrew, "Jessica". I see the way she looks at you. Or should I say, looks "through" you. It could be different if you just gave me a chance, Brandon.

    I also love exploring LA and all the fun things it has to offer! Totes feel free to check out my Facebook and Instagram!
